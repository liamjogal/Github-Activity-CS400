Beginning (Liam)
Once upon a time a man came across a pill that had the power to make him great and successful. He thought there was no downsides to this pill, until he discovered that the pill also had devastating consequences. Ultimately, he decided to take this pill even though the future was unclear. 
Middle (Joseph)
At first, the pill had no down sides. The man created one of the most successful companies in the world and becomes a millionare within only a few weeks. However, as the man became more successful, the more people began to dislike who he was. He became too powerful, too successful. He became corrupted, mistreated his workers and abused his power and wealth. Blind in his greed, he never knew what was about to hit him.
End (Jiaqi)
Eventually, some problems about his company were reported on the news. Small problems at first, but soon the negative news about him and his company was all over the place. The employees went on a massive protest against him. The investors began to sell off the stocks of his company. His partners turned to their competitors. Worst of all, his wife and kids left him. Within a week, he lost almost everything he had ever valued. It was then that he finnaly realized the devastating consequences of the magic pill. However, his regret cannot change anything.
